Privacy Policy

Matt's Automation Scripts — Last updated April 25, 2025

Overview

This privacy policy describes how Matt's Automation Scripts ("we", "us") collects and uses information submitted through the SMS alert opt-in form.

Information We Collect

We collect the mobile phone number you provide when opting in to receive SMS alerts. No other personal information is collected.

How We Use Your Information

Your phone number is used solely to send you automated SMS notifications triggered by personal automation scripts. These alerts may include status updates, task completions, and other event-driven notifications.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, share, or disclose your phone number or any other personal information to third parties. Your information is never used for marketing purposes.

Message Frequency & Rates

Message frequency varies based on script activity. Message and data rates may apply.

Opt-Out

You may opt out of SMS alerts at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive. Reply HELP for assistance.

Data Retention

Your phone number is retained only as long as you remain opted in. Upon opting out, your number is removed and no further messages will be sent.

Contact

For questions about this policy, please reply HELP to any message you receive.
